Alone in the woods

Getting up, patting your body to see you're all right, your mind is getting occupied by a whimsical surge of fireflies soaring around your body.

What is this weird place, I've never seen so many fireflies before, and definitely not this close to my body. They actually feel pretty warm, like they're actually burning. Seems like they're trying to show me something, they're forming a line to the path over there..
