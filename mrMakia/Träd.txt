To declare an object in my language.

-To mark the starts and the ends in my language we use 0
-When we want to declare our type, we list the type like this: 0Bil0 0Buss0
-To list our attributes we list them inside the # #, after the type.
-And to hold attributes apart we can use: |

When we mark up my language: 0Palle#old|slow#Mom#sweet|kind#Sister#evil|mean#0
